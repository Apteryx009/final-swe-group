What are some high protein foods?

In this post, we're going to take a look at some common high protein foods.

Eggs

Eggs are an excellent source of protein. The average egg contains 6-7g of protein. Eggs are also a source of other excellent vitamins and minerals.

Chicken

Chicken is an amazing source of protein.
100g of chicken contains 27g of protein, which is amazing. Bodybuilders love chicken, because of how high in protein it is, and how low in calories it is. 100g of chicken contains 165 calories, which is very low.

Greek Yogurt

Yogurt is another amazing source of protein.
A 3/4 cup of greek yogurt contains, on average, 18-21g of protein, with only 110-120 calories.

Cottage cheese

Cottage cheese is another dairy product with excellent nutrition.
One cup of cottage cheese contains 28g protein, with only 163 calories.
Some people like to combine it with protein powder, making for an even more high protein snack. Others will eat it as is, with a little salt, or blend it to make it smooth, and mix it in with other foods.
