How often do I need to work out?

In short, it's really up to you.


There's more than one way to work out. If you want to work out every day, you can, and if you want to work out once or twice a week, you can do that as well.

Personally, I work out every day to manage my ADHD symptoms, but at the very minimum, you only have to work out each muscle group once per week.

If you want to take a maximal approach rather than a minimal approach, meaning you work out as much as you possibly can, you can perform up to 10-20 sets per muscle group, per week. Going beyond this number is not advisable, as past a certain point, your body doesn't have the ability to properly recover from this volume of workout.

As with everything in life, it's up to you. If you like working out, you can work out more often, as long as you're recovering properly. If you're less enthusiastic about working out, you can keep the workouts down to once or twice a week.
