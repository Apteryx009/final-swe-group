Is cardio killing your gains?

Probably not.


Muscle growth is performed via muscle-protein synthesis, so as long as you're inn a caloric surplus, and you're getting enough protein, you will be gaining muscle.

Experts recommend that if you're trying to gain muscle, you should be eating 1.2-1.7 grams of protein per kilogram, or, if you use measuring systems that make don't sense, 0.5-0.8 grams of protein per pound.
